Lineup Coachella

The Coachella 2025 music festival, held over two weekends in April (April 11–13 and 18–20) at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California, featured an impressive and diverse lineup. The headliners included Lady Gaga on Friday, Green Day on Saturday, and Post Malone on Sunday, with Travis Scott joining as a special guest performer. Notable acts throughout the weekends included Missy Elliott, Charli XCX, Megan Thee Stallion, and BLACKPINK members Lisa and Jennie, who performed solo sets. Other big names included FKA Twigs, MARINA, The Go-Go’s, Zedd, Japanese Breakfast, Tyla, Clairo, and The Original Misfits. There were also exciting performances from Ed Sheeran, Weezer, Kraftwerk, The Prodigy, Rema, Yeat, Three 6 Mafia, and electronic acts like Tiësto, Amelie Lens, Infected Mushroom, Vintage Culture, and a collaboration between The Martinez Brothers and Loco Dice. The festival also had some unforgettable moments, such as Lady Gaga bringing out Gesaffelstein, Benson Boone performing with Brian May, and Charli XCX being joined by Troye Sivan, Lorde, Billie Eilish, and Addison Rae across both weekends. Post Malone shared the stage with Jelly Roll and Ed Sheeran, while Megan Thee Stallion collaborated with Courtney LaPlante, Queen Latifah, Victoria Monét, and Ciara. Regional Mexican artist Junior H brought out Peso Pluma and Tito Double P, and Shaboozey featured Noah Cyrus during his set. This year’s Coachella highlighted genre diversity, special guest surprises, and unforgettable collaborations that made it one of the most memorable editions yet.
